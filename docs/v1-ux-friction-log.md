# V1 UX friction log

Use during and after QA. Copy rows into the **UX friction** tab of the [v1 test tracker](v1-test-tracker-template.md) or track issues in your bug tracker.

| Date | Route / flow | Role | Observation | Severity (1–5) | Owner |
|------|--------------|------|-------------|----------------|-------|

**What to capture**

- Where users **got stuck** (blocked state unclear, missing empty state).
- Flows that felt **confusing** (too many steps, jargon).
- **State breaks** (UI shows success but DB not updated, or vice versa).
- **Unclear data** (KPIs, labels, dates, permissions).
